Printmaking

Through my year of printmaking experience, I've thoroughly enjoyed exploring themes of magic and self-discovery. My printmaking style is a fusion of fantasy-filled aesthetics and imagery that tells a story on an emotional level. I am trying to stray away from my black and white compositions and explore how color can add emotion in my most recent works.
