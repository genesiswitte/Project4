Painting

Through the play of light, shadow, and color, I aim to not just capture faces but emotional resonance with my work. With my acrylic paintings, I love to paint from photographs of moments that hold special meaning to me. Realism becomes a path to emotional connection, and my canvas becomes a visual diary of people and moments I love.
